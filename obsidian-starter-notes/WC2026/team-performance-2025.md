# Team Performance & Form — 2025-26 Season Summary

## Top Nations Club Season Summary

### Real Madrid (Spain)
- La Liga: Title race with Barcelona; Mbappe 24G LaLiga, Vinicius 19G/11A
- Champions League: Progressed to SF
- Key: Mbappe + Vinicius + Bellingham + Rodrygo — four WC stars in one club

### Barcelona (Spain/Brazil)
- La Liga: Title contenders with Real Madrid
- Raphinha (Brazil, 21G), Yamal (Spain, 16G/11A), Pedri (Spain) all starring
- Three WC-squad players among their best performers

### Bayern Munich (Germany)
- Bundesliga: Champions for 34th time
- Musiala: returned Jan 2026 after broken leg; 10 goal involvements since return
- Kane: 27 goals in Bundesliga — still world class
- Musiala WC fitness: 5 months since return — approaching full sharpness

### Arsenal (England)
- PL: Title contenders
- Saka: 18G/14A — PL's best winger
- Havertz: 16G — best season of career
- Rice + Saka + Havertz: three pillars of England's WC squad

### Liverpool (England/Germany)
- PL: Title winners
- Wirtz (Germany): 14G/11A — adapted instantly after €125M move Jun 2025
- Salah (Egypt): 22G/14A — still PL's best at 33 (called up Egypt squad)
- Van Dijk (Netherlands): commanding CB; Liverpool captain

### Inter Milan (Italy)
- Serie A: Title contenders
- Lautaro (Argentina): 23G — top scorer
- Thuram (France): 18G — second striker class

### PSG (France)
- Ligue 1: Champions easily
- Dembele (France): 14G/13A — creative hub
- Hakimi (Morocco): 5G/14A — best RB in world
