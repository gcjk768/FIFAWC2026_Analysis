# Head-to-Head Records — WC2026 Context

## Argentina vs France
- 2022 WC Final: Argentina won 4-2 (pens) after 3-3 AET (Mbappe hat-trick)
- 2018 WC R16: France won 4-3 (Mbappe brace)
- Historical: 9 meetings, Argentina 4W 2D 3L

## Brazil vs Germany
- 2014 WC SF: Germany 7-1 (Brazil's worst ever result)
- 2002 WC Final: Brazil 2-0 Germany
- Historical: Brazil 12W 5D 7L vs Germany

## Spain vs Morocco
- 2022 WC R16: Morocco 3-0 pens (0-0 AET) — biggest WC upset since 2022
- Historical advantage: Spain, but Morocco beat them last meeting

## France vs Morocco
- 2022 WC SF: France 2-0 Morocco
- Morocco never beaten France in major tournament

## England vs USA
- 2022 WC Group: 0-0 draw
- Historical: England advantage but USA held them last WC

## Brazil vs France

## Germany vs Japan
- 2022 WC Group: Japan 2-1 Germany (massive upset)

## Spain vs Japan

## Portugal vs Morocco

## Argentina vs Australia

## Netherlands vs Argentina
